Flowable argues that high-stakes, unpredictable work such as claims investigations needs agentic case management, where AI agents handle consequential decisions while remaining governed, auditable, and accountable. The post uses scenarios involving multiple parties, conflicting documentation, fraud signals, human judgment, and regulatory deadlines to show why adaptive case management beats rigid workflows.
